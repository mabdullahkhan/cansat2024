CanSat System

The CanSat system is an educational tool designed to simulate satellite payloads for hands-on learning in aerospace engineering.

It comprises an ESP8266 microcontroller, sensors including ultrasonic, MPU6050, and BMP380, as well as actuators like servo motors and a buzzer.

Ultrasonic Sensor

Used for altitude measurement.

MPU6050

Accelerometer/gyroscope for orientation sensing.

BMP380

Altitude and pressure sensor.

Servo Motors

Used for controlled descent.

Buzzer

Indicates landing.
